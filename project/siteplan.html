Siteplan

Site Name

loveofnailz.com

This name was chosen because my wife's business is called fortheloveofnailz. I just simplified it because it was a little too long.

Site Purpose

This site will be very friendly and fast, not only from users who use it from a computer, but also from a mobile. It will help customers to find things quick and be attracted by the design which will create a desire to make an appointment or buy from the the future store that will be added later, and also is for my wife to have less work and instead of using an automated website with things she doesn't like. She will have the option to choose how it looks.
